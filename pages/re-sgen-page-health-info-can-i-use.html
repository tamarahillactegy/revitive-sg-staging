Can I Use Revitive?

Who can’t use Revitive?

Revitive should not be used by persons

  • In the first trimester of pregnancy
  • Fitted with a pacemaker or AICD (automatic implantable cardioverter-defibrillator) - Revitive could interfere with the device
  • Being treated for existing deep vein thrombosis - to avoid any possibility of mobilising the clot

Local warnings and cautions

You should not use the Revitive device, Revitive TENS Body Pads or Revitive circulation gloves on or near:

  • Pregnant womb/stomach
  • Open, broken or bleeding skin – dress any open wounds before use
  • Tissue bleed (internal / external)
  • Epilepsy – do not use the TENS Body Pads on the neck area.
  • Malignancy (tumour)
  • Eyes
  • Testicles
  • Infected tissue (inc. Cellulitis)

If you have any concerns or would like further information you can call our UK call centre on 0845 871 94 94 or consult your doctor.
