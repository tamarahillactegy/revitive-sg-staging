The history of Revitive and Circulation Booster

Since 2005, our promise has been to deliver quality medical devices people will want to use. They must be:

  • Intuitive - simple to use
  • Enjoyable and fits easily into our daily routine
  • Value for money, and
  • Supported by research

Revitive IX - 2013

We are very proud of our award winning Revitive IX. It represents our biggest achievement in design and functionality.

Revitive IX won the 2012 Red Dot design award over 4500 other health products.

Health products don’t need to be boring – they need to inspire!

Circulation Booster v3 - 2009

The Circulation Booster v3 introduced the iconic round shape. We also reduced the size to ensure it could fit easily around the home and on the pharmacy shelf.

Circulation Booster V2 - 2007

“V2” introduced an LED display, optimised footpads and improved usability.

2007 was the year we began our long standing research relationship with Kings College Hospital and London South Bank University which continue today.

Circulation Booster

The first Circulation Booster was launched in 2005 in Australia via leading pharmacies, and in the UK at consumer shows such as the Ideal Home Show
